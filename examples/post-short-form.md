# Example: Post a Short/Reel/TikTok

## Your Prompt

```
Post this video as a YouTube Short, Instagram Reel, and TikTok.
[drag and drop your video file into the chat]
```

## What Claude Does

1. **Transcribes** your video to understand the content
2. **Creates unique captions** for each platform:
   - YouTube: Professional tone, detailed description, SEO tags
   - Instagram: Casual, 1-3 sentences, minimal hashtags
   - TikTok: Punchy, 1-2 sentences, trending hashtags
3. **Generates 3 title options** for YouTube Shorts
4. **Shows the full content package** for your review:

```
--- YOUTUBE SHORTS ---
Title options:
  1. "Stop Wasting Time on Social Media â€” Do This Instead"
  2. "The AI System That Posts for You (Free Setup)"
  3. "How I Post to 13 Platforms in 30 Seconds"

Description:
[Full YouTube description with hooks, links, hashtags]

Tags: [AI social media, content automation, ...]
First comment: "What platform do you spend the most time on? Drop it below."

--- INSTAGRAM REELS ---
Caption:
I replaced my social media routine with one AI conversation. Save this.

#aisocialmedia #contentcreation #automation

First comment: "Save this and send it to a creator who needs it."

--- TIKTOK ---
Caption:
2 hours on social media? I do it in 30 seconds. Link in bio.

#ai #socialmedia #contentcreator #automation #free
```

5. You pick a YouTube title and approve
6. Claude **uploads the video** to Late storage
7. Claude **posts to all 3 platforms** in one API call
8. Returns post URLs for each platform

## Result

Your video is live on YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok with unique, optimized content per platform. Total time: ~2 minutes (mostly waiting for upload).
