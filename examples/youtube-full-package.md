# Example: Full YouTube Upload

## Your Prompt

```
I want to upload this video to YouTube. Create a full content package for it.
[drag and drop your video file]
```

## What Claude Does

### Phase 1: Analysis
1. **Checks video size** — compresses if over 500MB
2. **Extracts transcript** with timestamps
3. **Analyzes content** — identifies key topics, structure, and takeaways

### Phase 2: Content Package
4. **Creates 5 title options:**
   ```
   1. "How to Build an AI Sales Campaign in Minutes (Full Demo)"
   2. "I Let AI Run My Sales Outreach — Here's What Happened"
   3. "The Free AI System That Books Meetings on Autopilot"
   4. "Stop Cold Calling. Build This AI Campaign Instead."
   5. "AI Sales Campaign: From Zero to Booked Calls (Step-by-Step)"
   ```

5. **Creates SEO-optimized description:**
   ```
   Build your first AI sales campaign for FREE
   https://yoursite.com/free-resource

   In this video, I show you exactly how to create a fully automated
   AI sales campaign from scratch. No complex tools. No coding. Just
   talk to the AI, and it builds everything for you.

   My Links:
   Subscribe: https://youtube.com/@yourchannel
   LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/yourprofile

   Timestamps:
   0:00 What We're Building
   0:37 The System Overview
   1:37 Dashboard Tour
   3:11 Setting Up the AI
   10:10 Campaign Generated
   17:08 Launch & Results

   #AIMarketing #SalesCampaign #Automation
   ```

6. **Researches tags:**
   ```json
   ["AI sales", "sales campaign", "AI marketing", "get clients",
    "book appointments", "agency owner", "sales automation",
    "AI agent", "business growth", "lead generation"]
   ```

7. **Creates first comment CTA:**
   ```
   Want to build your own AI sales campaign?
   https://yoursite.com/free-resource
   Set up your campaign in minutes. Drop a comment if you try it.
   ```

### Phase 3: Review & Post
8. **Asks for your thumbnail** (upload or generate concept)
9. **Shows complete package** for your approval
10. **Uploads video** to Late storage
11. **Posts to YouTube** with all fields (title, description, tags, firstComment, thumbnail)
12. **Returns the YouTube URL**

## Result

Your video is live on YouTube with a fully optimized package — title, description, tags, timestamps, first comment, and thumbnail. Total time: ~5 minutes.
